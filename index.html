Our Story

A Place Where Every
Cup Feels Like Home

Hangout Cafe was born from a simple dream — to create a space where people can slow down, savour good food, and enjoy great company. Nestled in the heart of the city, we've crafted an environment that blends warmth, creativity, and community.

From our artisan coffee brewed fresh every morning to our hand-prepared snacks made with the finest local ingredients, every detail at Hangout Cafe is designed with love. Come as a stranger, leave as family.
